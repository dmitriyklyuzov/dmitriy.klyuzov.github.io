What we do

Business law

Usten Law advises businesses, we counsel businesses during all stages of their lifecycles: from formation to operation, to expansion via merger or acquisition, to dissolution. As a mentor to startups, we work with seed and early-stage companies and startups, helping them refine business, financing and technology strategies to help enable rapid growth.

Civil Litigation

Usten Law, P.C. will take you through the entire process of filing and pursuing a non-criminal lawsuit. An attorney can also advise you on whether your case is best suited for court, or can be handled better outside of the courtroom.

Personal Injury

Usten Law are a team of experienced attorneys and staff who help you secure long term justice. The insurance companies make their billions by denying or minimizing your claims. Our job is to help ensure that they treat you fairly.

Real estate

Our law firm is known for its in-depth knowledge of the New York real estate market. This level of understanding, combined with our sound business sense, allows us to exceed our clients' expectations, however complex their case may be.
